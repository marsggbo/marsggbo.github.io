## 关于这件事 / About This

作为研究者，我一直相信：**好的研究如果没人看得懂，等于没做。**

As a researcher, I believe: **great research that nobody can understand is research wasted.**

从读博开始，我就坚持用中文写技术文章，把论文里的数学、代码、工程细节「翻译」成普通人能读懂的语言。这件事我做了将近 10 年，覆盖 AutoML、NAS、LLM 推理优化等方向。

Since my PhD, I've been writing Chinese tech articles — translating the math, code, and engineering details of papers into something readable. Nearly 10 years, covering AutoML, NAS, and LLM inference optimization.
