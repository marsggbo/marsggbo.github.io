## 项目背景 / Background

基于 **ExpertFlow**（DAC 2026）的核心技术，与国内头部科技企业及高校开展联合研究，探索稀疏 MoE 推理优化在真实生产环境中的落地路径。

Building on the core techniques of **[ExpertFlow (DAC 2026)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.17954)**, we conducted joint research with leading industry partners and universities in China, exploring how sparse MoE inference optimization translates from research to production deployment.

---

## 合作方向 / Collaboration Scope

- **大规模 LLM 集群推理优化** / Large-scale LLM cluster inference optimization  
  将 ExpertFlow 的专家预测与 Token 调度技术适配到工业级 GPU 集群，针对真实业务流量进行调优。  
  Adapting ExpertFlow's expert prediction and token scheduling to industrial-grade GPU clusters under real-world traffic patterns.

- **算法落地验证** / Algorithm-to-production validation  
  在实际部署环境中验证学术方法的可行性，处理生产环境中的工程挑战（批量调度、内存管理、容错等）。  
  Validating academic methods in real deployment environments, addressing engineering challenges in production (batch scheduling, memory management, fault tolerance).
